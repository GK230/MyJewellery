Geeta Kotecha

I have had a childhood ambition to design jewellery. I think I was inspired by my parents Indian jewellery business, however I have always loved making patterns and working with delicate things.

I trained for a few months at Holts Academy (now the British Academy of Jewellery) in Hatton Garden, and then later completed the City Lit Jewellery Level 1.
